About


The Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad (IIIT-A) was established in 1999, as a center of excellence in Information Technology and allied areas. The institute was conferred "Deemed University" status by Govt. of India in the year 2000. The Institute thus became empowered to have a perpetual seal and award degrees subsequent to the conduct of its own examinations. With the passage of IIIT Bill 2014 by the Parliament of India, IIIT Allahabad, along with the other 4 MHRD funded IIITs, became an Institute of National Importance on December 1, 2014.

Ranking


It is a matter of great pride for IIITians that in three academic rankings published recently, IIIT Allahabad has done excellently. Chronologically, the first of these was the 'QS University Rankings BRICS 2014', this ranks universities in the BRICS countries, namely, Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa.The ranking was made public on 18 June.

In this ranking, IIIT Allahabad was ranked 109, our ranking in India was 16. Then came the India Today ranking, where got the 25th rank in the list of India's top engineering institutes. Most recent is the Outlook ranking which places us at the 17th position among India's Top 75 Engineering Colleges.
